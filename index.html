About Calavera

Calavera Mexican Grill is Qaraoun's destination for authentic, bold, and fresh Mexican street food. Our culinary journey is built on passion and the belief that great Mexican food starts with the finest, freshest ingredients. From our vibrant, house-made salsas and creamy guacamole to our signature Loaded Mexican Fries and Calavera Specials, every item is crafted to deliver an unforgettable taste of Mexico. We bring the fiesta to your plate every day!
